About the project

A game that makes typing fun, Rally Typer combines the traditional and widely beloved race kart genre with a typing speed competition. The way it works is simple: you type out a pre-set text on your keyboard, aiming for perfect accuracy and exceptional speed. The faster and more accurate that you are, the better that you will do in the race. This is a browser-based game, so you don't need any peripheral hardware to play it. The graphics are cartoony and 3D, and the gameplay itself is extremely exciting, turning the simple act of typing into something frenetic and competitive.

When you type well in Rally Typer, you earn a turbo boost, and the "keyboard" boxes contain other weapons and boosts that will come in handy during gameplay. You can connect with your friends for social play, collect in-game currency for new karts and other upgrades, and display your kart collection in your virtual garage. Everything about Rally Typer emphasizes fast typing and frantic racing, and to make this game a reality, we are turning to you.
